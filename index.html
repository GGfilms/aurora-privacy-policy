Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2025

About Aurora Watch NZ

Aurora Watch NZ is a free app designed for users in New Zealand to monitor real-time aurora activity, KP index, solar wind data, and forecast visibility using trusted public data sources.

What Data We Use

  • Location: Your device’s location is used to show local aurora visibility, weather, and AI summaries. This data is never stored or shared.
  • Camera: Used only for the AR compass view to help orient toward the aurora. No photos or footage are recorded or transmitted.
  • Network: The app fetches live data from NOAA, OpenWeatherMap, and OpenAI APIs.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Aurora Watch NZ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, identifiers, or usage analytics.

Children

The app is safe for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
